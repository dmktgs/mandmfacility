About us

We are a trusted Facilities Management Providers
At M and M, we put safety and quality at the heart of our services. Our working practices underpin our daily operations, supporting our commitment to provide customer satisfaction and solution excellence. We strive to provide the best service possible for our clients – by working with us, you can focus on your business knowing that your workplace is safe and clean for your employees and visitors every day.
M&M will help you stay secure in the knowledge that your business is in good hands, leaving you more time to focus on the important aspects of your business. We can help with budgeting, allowing you to more accurately understand business maintenance costs every month.
Here at M&M Facilities Management, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your needs. Our multidisciplinary team of professionals have experience in delivering FM services to many different sectors, whether you require commercial & residential maintenance, public & private sector services or something else. We’re proud of our excellent reputation as a high-quality FM company who provide a range of clients with excellence time and time again.
MISSION
Our mission is to be the leading Facilities Management Services provider, most admired for delivering peace of mind, highest quality, and delivering value enhancements for owners and tenants.
VALUES
At M&M, our values are at the very core of who we are. Living our values every day makes a difference for our employees, customers and community where we live and work. With our values as a guide we will continue to pursue our mission to be the leading Facilities Management provider in India.
